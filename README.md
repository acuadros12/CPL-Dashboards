Business Dashboards Portfolio
* Overview
This repository showcases a collection of interactive dashboards I created during my time working at a company. These dashboards were designed to provide actionable insights across different areas of the business, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.
* Dashboards Included
- Sales Dashboards
Track revenue trends, product performance, and regional sales distribution.
Focused on identifying growth opportunities and monitoring KPIs.
- Inventory Dashboards
Monitor stock levels, turnover rates, and supply chain efficiency.
Built to optimize inventory management and reduce holding costs.
- Customer Analysis Dashboards
Explore customer demographics, behavior, and segmentation.
Designed to support retention strategies and targeted marketing.
- Accounts Receivables Dashboards
Visualize outstanding balances, aging reports, and payment trends.
Helped finance teams improve cash flow and collection efficiency.
* Tools & Technologies
- Power BI for interactive visualizations and business intelligence.
- SQL Server for data extraction and transformation.
- Python (pandas, scikit-learn) for advanced data cleaning and enrichment.
- Excel for quick analysis and prototyping.
* Purpose
These dashboards were created to:
- Provide clarity on key business metrics.
- Enable faster decision-making.
- Highlight trends and anomalies in sales, inventory, customers, and receivables.
*  How to Explore
Each folder contains:
- Dashboard screenshots.
- A brief description of the business problem addressed.
- Key insights and metrics visualized.
* Notes
- All data used in these dashboards has been anonymized or simulated to protect company confidentiality.
- This portfolio is intended to demonstrate my ability to design, build, and deliver impactful business intelligence solutions.
