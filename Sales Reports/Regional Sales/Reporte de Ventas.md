# Sales Report 2025 — Power BI Dashboard Portfolio

> **Tool:** Power BI Service &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Domain:** FMCG / Sales Analytics &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Pages:** 29

---

## Overview

End-to-end sales reporting solution built in Power BI for a consumer goods company. The report covers daily sales tracking, monthly targets, product mix, distributor and sales force performance, customer coverage, geographic breakdown, and variable compensation measurement — all connected to an Odoo ERP data source.
